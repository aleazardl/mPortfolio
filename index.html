About the Company

founded in 1990 as Southville International School (SIS) in a simple single-storey building (Elizalde Campus) meeting the basic requirements of the Department of Education, Culture and Sports (DECS). With two students and five teachers, SISC was initially recognized as a “Special School for the Intellectually Gifted.”SISC graduates have an excellent track record in gaining acceptance into top local and foreign universities. This long-standing tradition was started by the first two enrollees who studied Kinder up to High School at SISC. Both students eventually graduated with honors from top world-ranked universities in England.Grounded by its commitment to global excellence, Southville International School and Colleges (SISC) has no centuries of existence to take pride in as yet but its remarkable achievements speak of how it is now and in the years to come. SISC’s strength largely lies in academics most notably in the excellent passing rate of its courses with licensure exams, community service, innovative programs, and internationally-benchmarked curricula that have proven responsive to the needs of the students, the community, and the industry.

On hardware related, I checked the computer, speakers, mouse, keyboard and almost all classrooms in basic education and see if everything is working fine. I also helped install access points in tropical for ISAC in tropical campus. Some of these tasks were really challenging but is also fun to do at the same time. There were some that really annoyed me and drained me but it’s all just practice to what I’ll face soon and then I’ll be thankful that i still did it.

Problems Encountered while Performing the Task

I honestly had a lot of problems in any task that I had to perform in my early days at work and it’s because I always have problem adjusting to new environments and I actually had forgotten some of what I’ve learned from the past which affected my performance while doing or fixing something. In result, I had to keep asking questions, there even were times that I just searched for solutions on the internet because I was too shy to ask basic solutions to problems that I should already know. Some examples of what I’ve forgotten involves System unit parts and how to assemble it, Minor details about Network Management like what does an access point do, connecting a printer to another computer using the network or an IP address, etc. Some other problems that I’ve encountered is dealing with dusty areas and crouching while trying to fit myself when I had to go under the computer tables and connect unplugged USB, audio jack and most usually Ethernet. At the end of the day although I haven’t overcome all of this problems which resulted to me not fixing the problem, I still have tried and learned. And the amount of those I’ve accomplished still dominates that what I have not.

In my 2 and a half month of stay in the Management Information Systems department I have observed that with the help of my heads and co-practicums I have improved my skills particularly in communication and finding solutions to certain problems, things that I did not learn on my subjects.
